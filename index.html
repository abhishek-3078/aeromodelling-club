About Us

Founded in 2007 by Kamal Kant Gaur and co-founded by flying enthusiasts Dipesh, Rupesh, Suman and under able guidance of Dr.G.L Pahuja, the club has come afar since then. The love for aircraft, fervour to fly, apt juggle between the academics and hobby, perseverance, untiring labour, deft leadership and promising members has carried the legacy of excellence and made club stand this tall. Knowledge building through experiments with models and knowledge transfer to its members for sustaining the club is the main motto.

Activities

Designing and building the new models, rebuilding of the damaged models, flying the prepared models and ground pilot training to the budding pilots are the routine activities undertaken under the club

Event

The club organizes aeromodelling events during 'TECHSPARDHA' - national-level technical symposium of our college. RC aircraft making event famed as 'HIGH SKY' and hovercraft making event 'HOVER RACE' are the flagship events.

VTOL Vertical Takeoff And Landing, Quadcopters, Hexacopters, Hovercrafts, Hyperloop Pod, Flying Models including Gliders and BiPlanes.
